Welcome to Graham Checkout

Graham Checkout is a browser-based point-of-sale for classroom practice. Scan barcodes with a USB scanner or your device camera, build a cart, and pay with a test gift card.

How lookup works

  • Barcodes on the classroom inventory list are matched first—fast and works offline once the list has loaded.
  • Items you have scanned before are remembered on this device.
  • Otherwise the app looks up products online (Open Food, Beauty, and Products Facts, or Open Library for book ISBNs). Prices are generated for practice, not real retail prices.
  • If nothing matches, the app can auto-generate a plausible practice name and price, or you can switch to manual entry in Settings.
  • Open Admin to seed sample gift cards and demo products.
